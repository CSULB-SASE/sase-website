We are the Society of Asian Scientists & Engineers at California State University, Long Beach, committed to preparing leaders, celebrating diversity, and giving back to the Asian-American community.

Events & Highlights

SASE CSULB is a forerunner in elevating our students as evidenced by our successful events. From engineering workshops to our largest event to date: West Regional Conference 2020.

We are invested in contributing to student excellence at the collegiate level in order to build bridges among the student-led chapters on a national scale.
